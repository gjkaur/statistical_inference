*Source: Statistics, 11th Ed., Witte & Witte, Wiley 2017*

## Overview

Day 7 extends ANOVA to **repeated measures** and **two factors**, then introduces the **chi-square (χ²) test** for qualitative (nominal) data. Planned time: about **90 + 60 + 60 minutes**. Use the textbook to synergize your lecture.

---

## 1. Analysis of Variance — Repeated Measures (Chapter 17) — ~90 min

- Variance estimates
- Mean squares and the F ratio
- Table for the F distribution

---

## 2. Analysis of Variance — Two Factors (Chapter 18) — ~60 min

- Three F tests (main effects A and B, interaction A × B)
- Variance estimates
- Mean squares and F ratios
- Estimating effect size
- Multiple comparisons

---

## 3. Chi-Square Test for Qualitative Data (Chapter 19) — ~60 min

- One-variable χ² test (goodness-of-fit)
- Calculating χ²
- χ² distribution
- χ² test (including two-variable / independence as covered in the chapter)
