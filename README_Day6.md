## Overview

Day 6 covers the **t test for two related (paired) samples** and **one-factor analysis of variance (ANOVA)**. Each block is about 90 minutes. Use the textbook to support and deepen your lecture.

---

## 1. t Test for Two Related Samples (Chapter 15)

- Sampling distribution of the mean difference (D̄; δ in some texts)
- t test for two related samples
- Estimating effect size (e.g., Cohen’s d; confidence intervals)
- t test for the population correlation coefficient (ρ)

---

## 2. Analysis of Variance — One Factor (Chapter 16)

- Two sources of variability (between-group vs. within-group / error)
- F test
- Variance estimates and mean squares
- Mean squares and the F ratio (MS_between / MS_within)

### Supplemental points (synergize with the textbook)

- Table for the F distribution
- Multiple comparisons (e.g., Tukey’s HSD)
- Flow chart for ANOVA (overall F → decision → follow-up comparisons as appropriate)
